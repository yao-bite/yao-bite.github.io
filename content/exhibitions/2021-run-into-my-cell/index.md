### About the Exhibition

Me and my phone, my phone and me.

The phone is me, is a lover, is the place where your messages arrive. With a single phone as the prologue to the exhibition and the origin of a narrative on self-alienation, the lines between self and other, self and machine, subject and object blur into sameness. Only beyond the interface is there a chance to encounter the Other; or perhaps, even beyond the interface, there is only the self. My life is 100% desolation, 100% me.

Come meet your 100% self.
