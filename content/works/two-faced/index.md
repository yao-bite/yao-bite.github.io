![Photo by CHU Chi-Hung.](2022-twofaced-2024kingcar-7.tw.webp)

Image is a thin veil.

She becomes a two-faced or two-backed person, simultaneously inside the window and outside; when disappearing behind the curtain, she is neither inside nor outside. Through imagination, her body's position forms a concave negative space: within the "thickness" of the image, a "nowhere," a place that doesn't exist.

Simultaneously inside and outside, or not inside nor outside. The front without a back, and the back without a front. Upon departure, going nowhere.
